Grilled Bison Burger

Description

This grilled lean bison cheeseburger offers all the satisfaction of a classic beef burger with a healthier twist. Add your favorite toppings for a gourmet burger you can feel good about!

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound Ground Bison
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp Worchesterchire sauce
  • Hot sauce
  • Garlic
  • 4 Slices Cheese
  • 4 Hamburger Buns

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill to high heat and oil the grate
  2. Mix bison, , egg, Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, garlic, and pepper together in a bowl
  3. Form the mixture into patties
  4. Place patties on baking sheet, cover with foil, and refrigerate until chilled, about 15 minutes.
  5. Top each burger with a slice of cheese and wait until cheese is melted
  6. Use a thermometer to check if the inside temperature reaches 140F
  7. Serve the burgers on a serving platter
