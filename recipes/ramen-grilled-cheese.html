Ramen Grilled Cheese

Description

Ramen grilled cheese is a tasty and unique twist on the classic grilled cheese sandwich. This recipe combines the comforting flavors of ramen noodles and gooey melted cheese between two slices of bread. The instant ramen noodles are cooked and layered on top of a slice of bread with cheese, then topped with another slice of cheese and bread. The sandwich is then grilled until golden brown and the cheese is melted to perfection. The result is a crispy, savory, and satisfying sandwich that is perfect for a quick and easy lunch or dinner.

The ramen noodles add a unique texture and depth of flavor to the traditional grilled cheese, making it a perfect dish for those who love to experiment with new and exciting flavors. With its simplicity and delicious taste, the ramen grilled cheese recipe is sure to become a new favorite for anyone who loves comfort food with a twist.

Ingredients

  • 1 (3 ounce) package instant ramen noodles
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 slices sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Bring 2½ cups water to a boil in a small saucepan. Add noodles and cook for 2 minutes. Add flavor packet, stir, and continue to cook for another 30 seconds, or prepare ramen according to package directions. Drain off liquid; allow noodles to cool slightly.
  2. Whip egg in a mixing bowl until yellow and frothy. Stir in Parmesan cheese. Stir in cooked noodles until completely coated.
  3. Line a sealable sandwich container with plastic wrap, and press half of ramen noodles into the lined container. Place another layer of plastic wrap into the container. Press remaining noodles evenly into the container. Cover and refrigerate until firm, at least 15 hours.
  4. Place a skillet over medium-high heat and add a layer of parchment paper. Add the butter. Remove one piece of ramen “bread” from the container and place into the skillet.
